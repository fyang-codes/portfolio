Vita Verde Fitness Center

Brand identity and environmental design for an eco-conscious fitness facility.

Reasoning

The Vita Verde Fitness Center project emerged from a growing demand for sustainable, wellness-focused spaces that align with environmentally conscious values. This project aimed to create a cohesive brand identity and physical environment that promotes both personal and environmental health.

Process

The development process for this project included:

  • Market analysis of existing fitness facilities and sustainability practices
  • Stakeholder interviews to identify core values and vision
  • Mood board and brand concept development
  • Environmental design mockups with sustainable material specifications

Brand development process showing evolution from concept to final design system

Results

The completed project delivered a comprehensive brand package and design guidelines:

  • Complete visual identity system including logo, color palette, and typography
  • Environmental signage and wayfinding system
  • Marketing materials and announcement posters for grand opening
