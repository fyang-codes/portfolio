Women in Legislation

Visual campaign highlighting women's contributions to legislative processes.

Reasoning

Despite significant progress, women remain underrepresented in legislative bodies worldwide. This project aims to increase visibility and awareness of women's contributions to policymaking and governance through a series of informational posters and digital content.

Process

The development of this visual campaign involved several key phases:

  • Research on historical and contemporary women legislators
  • Interviews with current women in legislative positions
  • Development of visual language and design system
  • Iterative design process with focus group feedback

Design process showing the evolution of poster concepts and visual elements.

Results

The campaign resulted in a series of visually striking educational materials:

  • Set of 12 announcement posters highlighting notable women legislators
  • Digital campaign that reached over 50,000 viewers
  • Educational resources adopted by three university political science departments

Final poster series displayed at legislative buildings and educational institutions.
