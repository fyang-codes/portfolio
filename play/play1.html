About This Project

As a personal project, I created a custom gift by sourcing a 3D model (.stl file) from Thingiverse, preparing it using PrusaSlicer, and executing the full 3D print process.

Final 3D printed custom gift

The Process

I optimized the print settings (layer height, supports, infill, etc.) for quality and reliability, and selected suitable materials for the final product. The project involved troubleshooting printing challenges and making adjustments to ensure a successful print.

What I've Learned

This project demonstrates my ability to work with open-source resources, troubleshoot printing challenges, and apply hands-on digital fabrication skills. I gained valuable experience in 3D printing techniques that can be applied to future prototyping and maker projects.
