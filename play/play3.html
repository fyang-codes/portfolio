Electronics and Soldering Project

About This Project

As a personal learning project, I assembled a festive LED Christmas tree ornament from a soldering kit. This involved hands-on work with electronic components such as resistors, LEDs, capacitors, and switches.

Key Skills Developed

  • Soldering Techniques: Gained proficiency in using a soldering iron safely and effectively to connect components on a PCB.
  • Circuit Assembly: Followed a schematic to understand component placement and circuit logic while assembling the board step by step.
  • Testing & Troubleshooting: Verified circuit functionality and diagnosed issues such as cold joints or reversed components, building problem-solving skills in basic electronics.
