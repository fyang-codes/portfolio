Exploring Complex Societal Challenges

Interdisciplinary Research Fellow - Honors Program

Documentary: "De Eerste" - Farmer Perspectives on the Nitrogen Crisis

Reasoning

Led comprehensive research into one of the Netherlands' most pressing environmental challenges as part of an elite interdisciplinary honors program, producing original content that bridges academic research with public discourse.

Process

My interdisciplinary research approach involved multiple methodologies:

  • Investigated societally relevant environmental policy through multi-stakeholder analysis of the Dutch nitrogen crisis
  • Applied advanced interdisciplinary research methodologies including field observation, cross-sector integration, and scientific communication
  • Engaged with subject matter experts across Utrecht University through intensive seminar participation
  • Produced and published "De Eerste," a documentary featuring authentic farmer perspectives on environmental policy (available on YouTube)

Results

This project successfully bridged academic research with real-world perspectives, creating an accessible documentary that highlighted farmer voices in the nitrogen policy debate. The work demonstrated the value of interdisciplinary approaches to complex societal challenges.

This project represents one component of a comprehensive honors program that included personal reflection work, systems thinking exercises, and collaborative research initiatives.
