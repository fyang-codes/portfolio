What Are Dutch Farmers Up To? (Responding to the Nitrogen Crisis)

Interdisciplinary Research Fellow - Honors Program

Documentary Creator: "De Eerste" - Available on YouTube

The Challenge

Deep dive into one of the Netherlands' most complex challenges through an elite honors program. Spoiler alert: farming and environmental policy is way more complicated than anyone thinks.

The Journey

  • Tackled real-world environmental challenges that actually matter to Dutch society
  • Learned to think like a scientist, anthropologist, and communicator all at once
  • Picked the brains of experts from every corner of Utrecht University in intense seminar sessions
  • Created "De Eerste" - a documentary giving farmers a voice in the nitrogen debate (yes, it's on YouTube and people actually watch it)

Results

By documenting authentic farmer perspectives on the nitrogen crisis, we created a bridge between academic policy discussions and the lived experiences of those most affected. The documentary provided a platform for voices often excluded from policy debates.

Part of a comprehensive honors program that also included deep personal reflection work and collaborative research projects - basically academic boot camp for curious minds.
