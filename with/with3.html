The Challenge

Sometimes the best leadership happens when you just start doing things. I decided IFMSA-Utrecht needed some life, so I gave it some life.

What Happened

  • Launched the first-ever meditation workshop for Medicafe (because stressed med students need zen too)
  • Threw myself into events and activities until IFMSA-Utrecht started buzzing again
  • Collaborated with amazing teammates to create programs that people actually wanted to join
  • Helped transform a sleepy organization into something students genuinely got excited about

Results

What started as a simple initiative to bring more wellness programming to medical students evolved into a broader revitalization of IFMSA-Utrecht. By introducing fresh ideas and energy, we sparked renewed interest and engagement throughout the organization.
