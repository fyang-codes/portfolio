The Beautiful Chaos

Ever tried herding cats? Try organizing trips with Dutch students. From Gouda cheese tours to Efteling adventures, I somehow managed to get everyone to the right place at the right time (mostly).

The Beautiful Chaos

  • Coordinated epic adventures across the Netherlands - Gouda, Zwolle, Groningen, Ameland, Efteling, and more
  • Mastered the art of group emails that people actually read, restaurant negotiations, and digital organization that didn't collapse
  • Became the meeting minutes queen and the to-do list guru (someone had to keep track of everything)
  • Created feedback forms that actually improved things - revolutionary concept, I know

Results

Somehow we turned chaos into memorable experiences that students still talk about. The trips not only introduced international students to Dutch culture but fostered lasting friendships and created a stronger sense of community among participants.
